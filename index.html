Our Story

From the Forests of
Nilambur to Your Home

Nilamburwoods was born from a simple belief — great furniture shouldn't be hard to find. We work directly with craftsmen in the Nilambur valley, bringing their finest work to families in Thiruvananthapuram who value quality over quantity.

We're not a store. We're a bridge — between forest and home, between artisan and owner. Every listing on this platform has been personally vetted. Every piece carries a story worth telling.

Come visit. See the collection. Take your time. Good furniture waits for the right home.
